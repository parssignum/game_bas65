
- Press the SPACE button to throw
   the ball
   
- Press the LEFT and RIGHT arrows 
   to guide the paddle
   
- Press UP to fire bullets, which
   are used to destroy brown blocks.
   All other blocks may be destroyed
   with the ball. Be cautious: bullets
   bounce back and harm the paddle

- If all blocks are cleared you will 
   advance to the next level
   
- If the ball falls off the bottom of 
   the screen or the paddle loses all
   health points you lose a life

- Win by completing all levels before 
   running out of lives
