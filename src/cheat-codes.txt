
 1-5: advance to a specific level

 G: activate "God Mode", where the ball 
      will never fall of the screen and 
      the paddle is invincible

 R: reset the ball and paddle to their 
      initial positions

 N: destroy everything on screen and 
      fail immediately

 S: activate the Speed power-up 
      (faster paddle)
      
 M: activate the Multiply power-up 
      (spawn more balls)
      
 T: activate the Slow Time power-up 
      (decrease ball speed)
